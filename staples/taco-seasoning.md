# Taco Seasoning #

## Ingredients ##

* 1 Tbsp Onion Powder
* 1 Tbsp Cumin
* 1 Tbsp Chili Powder
* 1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika
* 1/2 Tbsp Garlic Powder
* 3 tsp Kosher Salt
* 1 tsp Ancho Powder
* 1 tsp Chipotle Powder
* 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
* 

## Preparation ##

1. Put it all in a vessel,
2, Mix thoroughly.
