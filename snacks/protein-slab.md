# The Modular Protein Brick System
*A zero-sugar, high-density protein ration designed for maximum jaw effort and satiety.*

## 1. The Base Ration (Foundational Structure)
*Prepare this first. It provides the "Barebell-style" doughy-but-dense texture.*

* **1/2 cup** Unflavored Micellar Casein
* **1/2 cup** Unflavored Whey Isolate
* **2 tbsp** Coconut Flour (Structural binder)
* **3 tbsp** Healthy Fat Binder (Tahini for savory / Almond Butter for sweet)
* **1 tsp** Beef Gelatin Powder (Optional: adds "rubbery" resistance/gristle feel)

---

## 2. "Total Zero" Savory Variation
*Best for: Maximum umami with absolute zero added sodium or potassium.*

**Add these to the Base Ration:**
* **2 tbsp** Nutritional Yeast (Verify 0mg Sodium on label)
* **2 tbsp** Mushroom Powder (Shiitake or Porcini)
* **1 tbsp** Onion Powder
* **1 tsp** Cracked Black Pepper (Provides the "bite" usually given by salt)
* **1 tsp** Apple Cider Vinegar (Essential to "brighten" the umami)
* **2-3 tbsp** Warm Water (To bloom the mushroom powder)

---

## 3. "Potassium-Enhanced" Savory Variation
*Best for: A sharper, traditional "salty" taste while avoiding sodium.*

**Add these to the Base Ration:**
* **2 tbsp** Nutritional Yeast
* **1 tbsp** Mushroom Powder
* **1/2 tsp** Potassium Chloride (Salt Substitute)
* **1/2 tsp** Garlic Powder & Smoked Paprika
* **2-3 tbsp** Warm Water 

---

## 4. Sweet Variation: "The 1980s Carob-Nostalgia"
*Best for: A dense, dessert-style brick without the sugar spike.*

**Add these to the Base Ration:**
* **2 tbsp** Raw Cacao Powder (or Carob powder)
* **1 tsp** Cinnamon
* **10-15 drops** Liquid Monk Fruit or Stevia
* **1/4 cup** Crushed Walnuts (Optional: adds "bone-like" crunch)
* **2-3 tbsp** Unsweetened Almond Milk

---

## General Instructions
1. **Bloom:** Mix your powders (Mushroom/Potassium/Cacao) into your liquid first.
2. **Combine:** Whisk dry ingredients, then add the fat binder and the liquid slurry.
3. **Process:** Use a food processor or stiff spatula until it reaches a "heavy clay" consistency.
4. **Compress:** Press into a lined container with significant force to remove air pockets.
5. **Cure:** Refrigerate for 4+ hours before slicing.
